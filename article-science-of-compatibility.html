The Science of Compatibility: How We Calculate Love

Dr. Amour ‚Ä¢ Feb 10, 2026 ‚Ä¢ 4 min read

Can love really be quantified? For centuries, humans have looked to the stars, numbers, and names to predict romantic destiny. While true love is complex and mysterious, our calculator combines ancient methods with modern algorithms to give you a glimpse into your potential connection.

The FLAMES Method

One of the most popular childhood games, F.L.A.M.E.S. stands for:

  • F - Friends
  • L - Lovers
  • A - Affection
  • M - Marriage
  • E - Enemies
  • S - Siblings

It works by removing common letters between two names and counting the remaining characters. This count is then used to eliminate letters from the word 'FLAMES' until only one remains. It's simple, nostalgic, and surprisingly insightful!

Numerology & Name Compatibility

Every letter carries a vibration. In numerology, names are converted into numbers (A=1, B=2, etc.) and reduced to a single digit "Soul Urge" number. Our percentage calculator uses a hashing algorithm inspired by this concept. It takes the unique character codes of your combined names to generate a consistent compatibility score.

Psychological Compatibility

Beyond names, true compatibility relies on shared values, communication styles, and life goals. The "Big Five" personality traits (Openness, Conscientiousness, Extraversion, Agreeableness, Neuroticism) are often used by relationship experts to predict long-term success. While our tool is for fun, it simulates the excitement of discovering that potential spark.

Did You Know?
Couples who laugh together stay together. Shared humor is one of the strongest indicators of relationship longevity according to a 2024 study by the Institute of Relationship Science.

So, whether you get 99% or "Enemies", remember that you are the author of your own love story. Calculators are fun, but your heart knows best.
